About

Biography

Hassan Ahmed currently serves as an Instructor in the Department of Computer Science at FAST-National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES), CFD Campus, Pakistan. In this role, he conducts undergraduate labs in core subjects such as Programming Fundamentals, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, and IT in Business. He actively contributes to the academic community as a member of the committee for the National Computing Education Accreditation Council (NCEAC). His work focuses on fostering a robust learning environment, integrating practical skills with theoretical knowledge, and supporting curriculum development to prepare students for real-world challenges in computer science.

Hassan holds a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Wah, Pakistan (2022–2024), where he developed DeepCAN: A Robust Deep Learning Framework for CAN Intrusion Detection in Autonomous Vehicles under the supervision of Professor Dr. Wazir Zada Khan. He earned a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering from COMSATS University Islamabad, Wah Campus (2018–2022). His undergraduate thesis, Lone Ranger - First Person Shooting Game, was supervised by Ms. Mehwish Mukhtar, showcasing his early expertise in software development. Prior to his current role, Hassan served as a Graduate Assistant at the University of Wah (2022–2024), where he conducted labs in Database Systems, Database Applications, and Applications of ICT, contributed to Outcome-Based Education (OBE) curriculum development, and supported departmental administrative tasks.

Hassan has made significant strides in research, particularly in deep learning and cybersecurity. His work on DeepCAN was published in the 2025 International Conference on Emerging Technologies in Electronics, Computing, and Communication (ICETECC). He has actively engaged in the academic community by presenting at conferences such as the 4th International Conference on Smart Plant Protection (2024) and the 2nd ICETECC (2025). Additionally, he has reviewed papers for prestigious events, including the International Conference on IT and Industrial Technologies (2024) and the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Green Computing (2025).

Hassan’s achievements have been recognized through numerous accolades. He received the Outstanding Contribution Award from the Prime Minister of Pakistan for the All Pakistan Minorities Heritage Photo Contest (2023). His vocal talents earned him multiple awards in national and inter-university competitions, including three wins in Naat Competitions at COMSATS University Islamabad, two wins in Kalam-e-Iqbal Competitions, and first and second positions in national Naat and Milli Naghma competitions organized by institutions like the Pakistan National Arts Council and FGEI (C/G).

Beyond academia, Hassan is passionate about photography, specializing in landscape, street, and portrait genres. His love for traveling and adventure fuels his creativity, as he enjoys exploring diverse cultures and traditions, enhancing his communication and interpersonal skills. An award-winning singer, Hassan has excelled in national and inter-university Naat and Kalam-e-Iqbal competitions, reflecting his deep connection to cultural and spiritual expression.

Hassan is actively seeking a PhD or RA position to advance his research in machine learning, explainable AI, and interdisciplinary applications, particularly in the area of Deep Learning, XAI, Medical Imaging, and Network Security. His goal is to contribute to innovative solutions that bridge computer science with real-world challenges.
